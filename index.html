Quick Reference

Key Terms — Defined Once, Used Throughout

PMT
Monthly payment. PMT = PV × r / (1 − (1+r)−n). Fixed amount covering both principal + interest each month.
PD — Probability of Default
Chance a borrower fails to repay. Base: 10% (conservative vs. Salmon's observed 6.5% DPD2+ on product loans).
LGD — Loss Given Default
Share of outstanding balance lost if default occurs. Base: 65% — standard unsecured SEA benchmark.
EAD — Exposure at Default
Outstanding balance at the time a borrower defaults. For declining-balance loans, avg EAD ≈ 50–60% of original loan. Critical for correct ECL.
ECL — Expected Credit Loss
ECL = PD × LGD × EAD. Uses average outstanding balance, not original loan amount — because borrowers repay principal over time before any default occurs.
CoF — Cost of Funds
Rate Salmon pays to source capital. Base: 8% p.a. (mature IFC+local blend). Year 1 pilot: 18–25% (equity-heavy; see Slide 8).
CAC — Customer Acquisition Cost
Total cost to originate one loan: agent commission + verification + marketing. Target: ≤₱2,000. Included in all our unit-econ calculations.
APR — Annual Percentage Rate
Annualised interest rate. 4.9%/mo nominal = ~76% effective APR. Bukas charges the same upper bound publicly.
DPD2+ / DPD30+
Days Past Due. DPD2+ = Salmon's current default signal on product loans (6.5%). DPD30+ = standard 30-day NPL threshold.
TAM / SAM / SOM
Total / Serviceable / Obtainable market. TAM = 5.2M students. SAM = 2.5M. SOM = 55–83K/yr (bottom-up; see Slide 4).
TVET / TESDA / CHED
Technical-Vocational Ed & Training / its regulator / Commission on Higher Education. Three separate student populations.
RA 10931 / HB 2589 / HB 9275
Free Tuition Law (SUCs) / ₱15B Student Loan Fund bill / Proposed 8% monthly interest rate cap. Last one is our key regulatory risk.
